Common Mistakes

Why the advice you've been given
is probably making it worse

👑

Trying to Show Who's Boss

Alpha thinking escalates the very behaviour you're trying to fix. It creates fear - and fear is the root of guarding, not the solution to it.

✋

Taking Things by Force

Grabbing items away teaches your dog that humans are a threat. It confirms exactly what their guarding is based on: that things will be taken.

🤐

Punishing the Growl

The growl is your dog's warning signal. Suppress it and you remove the only visible sign before a bite. A dog that no longer growls hasn't got better - it's got silenced.

When your dog growls, they aren't trying to be bad. They are just trying to tell you they are scared. Once you understand that, everything gets easier.
- Shirin Dhabhar
